NAME GENERATOR

The Akans are a meta-ethnicity who primarily live in Ghana and Ivory Coast. They have a fairly unique naming system, which is based on the day in the week a child is born, and on the gender of the child. So a boy born on a Monday would be named Kwadwo , and a girl would be called Adwoa. Each day has one or more variants, but there's usually not much to choose from.

The Akan Name Calculator takes your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born and based upon you gender generates your unique Akan name.
